Analysis of Financial Recession

CS416 - Narrative Visualization Project

Overview:

The following set of visualizations display historical financial data that can be used to understand different aspects of economic recessions in the US. Some indicators of recession are: gross domestic product (GDP), unemployment rate, stock market performance, consumer spending, industrial production, and more. This narrative will focus on stock market data to help understand how this particual indicators was affected during the past couple recessions. The data will highlight the dot-com bubble (2000-2002), Great Recession (2007-2009), and Covid Recession (2020). By exploring these patterns, we can gain a comprehensive understanding of the current state of the US economy and predictfor future recessions.

Data from Kaggle
