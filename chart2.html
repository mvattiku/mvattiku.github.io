Analysis of Financial Recession Indicators

Overview:

The following set of visualizations display historical financial that can be used to understand different aspects of economic recessions in the US. Some indicators of recession are: gross domestic product (GDP), unemployment rate, stock market performance, consumer spending, industrial production, and more. This narrative will focus on GDP, unemployment rate, and stock market performance data to, help understand how these indicators were affected during the past couple recessions. The data will highlight the dot-com bubble (2001), Great Recession (2007-2009), and Covid Recession (2020). By exploring these patterns, we can gain a comprehensive understanding of the current state of the US economy and predictfor future recessions.
